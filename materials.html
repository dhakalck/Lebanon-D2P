📌 Pre-Training Preparation for Unit 2

Required Actions Before June 15

  1. Review Unit 1 Deliverables
    • Re-read your group's problem statement
    • Review identified policy options and stakeholder analysis
    • Familiarize yourself with data sources you've identified
  2. Download and Test Tools
    • Download all Unit 2 Excel templates
    • Ensure Microsoft Excel works properly on your laptop
    • Test that you can open and edit the templates
  3. Pre-Reading (Optional but Recommended)
    • Review Module L09 presentation on framing economic evaluation
    • Read the "10 Steps to Developing a Policy Brief" (Steps 6-9 focus on economics)
    • Review the economic evaluation glossary for key terms
  4. Prepare Data
    • Compile any cost data you've collected for your interventions
    • Identify probability data for health outcomes (if available)
    • Note any data gaps to discuss with mentors

Estimated preparation time: 3-4 hours
