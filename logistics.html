What to Bring

📱 Technology

  • Laptop with Microsoft Excel
  • Laptop power adapter and charging cable
  • Universal power adapter (Jordan uses Type C, D, F, G, J plugs, 230V)

📚 Materials

  • Unit 1 deliverables (problem statement, policy options, stakeholder analysis)
  • Any cost data or health outcome data you've collected
  • Notebook and pens
  • USB flash drive for backup
  • Business cards for networking

🎒 Personal Items

  • Valid passport
  • Travel and health insurance documents
  • Personal medications
  • Comfortable business casual attire
  • Light jacket (training rooms may be air-conditioned)

Currency and Expenses

Currency

The official currency in Jordan is the Jordanian Dinar (JOD). Exchange rate: approximately 1 JOD = 1.41 USD (rates vary).

Payment Methods

  • Credit cards widely accepted at hotels and restaurants
  • ATMs available throughout Amman
  • Cash recommended for taxis and small purchases
