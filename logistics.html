Travel Information

For Lebanese Participants

Flight Information

Participants are responsible for booking their own flights from Beirut to Amman. Common flight options include:

  • Royal Jordanian (direct flights)
  • Middle East Airlines (direct flights)
  • Flight time: Approximately 1 hour

Airport

Queen Alia International Airport (AMM)
Distance to W Amman Hotel: Approximately 45 minutes by car

Visa Requirements

Lebanese citizens can obtain a visa on arrival at Queen Alia International Airport. Requirements:

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Visa fee (approximately 40 JOD / $56 USD for single entry)
  • Hotel confirmation (W Amman reservation)

Airport Transfer

Participants are responsible for arranging transportation from the airport to the hotel. Options include:

  • Hotel shuttle (inquire when booking)
  • Taxi service
  • Ride-sharing apps (Uber, Careem available in Amman)

Accommodation

W Amman Hotel

The training will be held at W Amman Hotel. Participants may choose to stay at the hotel for convenience or select alternative accommodation in Amman.

Hotel Reservation: Participants are responsible for booking and paying for their own accommodation. Please make reservations well in advance to ensure availability.

Alternative Accommodation Options

Currency and Expenses

Currency

The official currency in Jordan is the Jordanian Dinar (JOD). Exchange rate: approximately 1 JOD = 1.41 USD (rates vary).

Payment Methods

  • Credit cards widely accepted at hotels and restaurants
  • ATMs available throughout Amman
  • Cash recommended for taxis and small purchases
